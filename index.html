Passion for Design

My interest in graphic design began while working at my family's cycling apparel company. I was fascinated as I watched their ideas come to life, and I quickly grew interest in building my own ideas into something that I could hold and admire in the real world.

They inspired me to purchase my first screen printing press, and I began to make t-shirts for myself. I loved the thrill of wearing a design that was created by my own hands, and my designs quickly spread to be enjoyed by others.
